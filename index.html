Anne Seo-young Lee

I am an Ph.D. student at Cornell University in the department of Human Centered Design . I am currently working in DUET lab and luckily I had an opportunity to work with Professor So-Yeon Yoon . I completed my B.A. in Communication at Cornell University and M.A. in Communication at Seoul National University (2019). I worked at Robotics and Media Institute as a researcher at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology .

My research interests are in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), especially, design evaluation employing emerging technologies to understand emotional, cognitive, and behavioral responses to design elements in physical and virtually built environments. I am also interested in applying Extended Reality (XR) as a design research tool.
